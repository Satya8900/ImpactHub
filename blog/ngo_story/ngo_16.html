Story

Ghaswala Vision Foundation has a take on how ImpactHub helped them as a volunteering platform and met their volunteering requirements.. Here is what they have to say - "I met a representative from ImpactHub at a workshop on Social Media. The interaction was delightful and we never thought we would get such a lot of help from ImpactHub 9 out of 10 volunteers that came to us from ImpactHub were best suited for the activity. We did find a few volunteers who truly related to the cause. We are basically a Volunteer based organisation and we are akin to "Uber of Eye Care". We do not have any infrastructure or employees and yet we Add Value to society by hand holding the Abandoned Poor. Volunteers have given us various kinds of assistance. From patient follow up calls to setting up social media pages and creating handles. Volunteers have also helped in designing an end-to-end cloud computing platform along with tips on data entry" DM or call us if you're an Ngo and are in search for such enthusiastic volunteers and we'll get you connected!! We surely recommend ImpactHub when it comes to collaborating for a social cause, and helping find the right set of volunteers."
