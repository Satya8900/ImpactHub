Shree Arbuda Residential Welfare Association (SARWA) - aims to work on improving the economic, social, and physical well-being of society, education, and child psychological development.

Published on 10 February, 2022



Story

Aims to work on improving the economic, social, and physical well-being of society, education, and child psychological development. They conduct various drives on awareness in schools and colleges and also conduct sessions to educate and sensitize people.

Here's what they have to say about their association with us -

"We got connected with ImpactHub in 2019 and from the beginning our requirements were very clear that we need volunteers for graphic designing and content writing.

ImpactHub understood our concerns pretty well and connected us with volunteers accordingly.

We have had a great set of volunteers who have worked with us before and are still helping us out in raising awareness about our initiatives, projects, fundraisers and more through social media!

ImpactHub made it super easy for us to handle our volunteering requirements.

They have been a great support from the beginning. Our social media and website have been uplifted through volunteers connected by them only.

We have got great volunteers who helped us in Graphic designing and content writing.

We got connected to one volunteer Aparna Desai who helped us in Graphic designing, website logo designing, marketing, social media(Instagram), and website content. Through her help, we are present and active on social media and after that, we got a really good response from 2 CSR companies as well through volunteers provided by ImpactHub."
