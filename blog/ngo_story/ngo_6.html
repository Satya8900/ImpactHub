Story

Our NGO partner, Urja Trust is an organization that works for young, homeless women, with a vision of a just, equitable, and compassionate society where young women pursue their lives with freedom and dignity. Founded in 2011, the Trust believes that the aid, development, and ultimate transformation of young homeless women entails not just economic sufficiency but also mental health stabilization. ImpactHub onboarded Urja Trust on 14th July 2016 and since then our association has been strengthened with 40+ volunteering opportunities and 250+ volunteers connected to this NGO. Read on as Charu from the Urja Charitable Trust speaks about their association with ImpactHub “We have had a great year with ImpactHub and had some wonderful volunteers who were connected with us. I would like to add that the team at ImpactHub is really great and forthcoming when it comes to helping us out, when we need any assistance they are always there with a helping hand. The connections have been great with professional people who helped Urja in various areas like Social Media, Graphic Designing, Content Writing. I look forward to having more wonderful years connecting with ImpactHub and the team which is associated with it.” We, at ImpactHub, thank you for your kind words and how you have always been super responsive to our volunteers and events.
