Pruthvi Shah did his bit by Volunteering as a Photographer and has impacted many young lives!

Published on 10 December, 2020



Story

Pruthvi Shah did his bit by Volunteering as a Photographer and has impacted many young lives! Here is what he has to say about his experience - "One fine day I was searching for a good opportunity to showcase my talent and to make my portfolio strong. Instagram was there but I had that hunger for more so I started surfing on Google and thenI came to know about ImpactHub. ImpactHub is one of the best platforms for volunteers as well as for NGOs to connect for a good cause. I am connected with one of the organizations called ARTSCAPE. Artscape works in the area of mental wellness and uses expressive arts therapy to design sessions that aim to imbibe positivity and well-being . I am really enjoying the experience over there because whenever i am volunteering it gives a sense of purpose to know exactly who was helped by your service. It has given me New Exposure to life. I am volunteering as a Photographer, my role is to cover session scenario and working closely with artscape team to develop the session concept" If you're a photographer/videographer who wants to showcase your talent and make a difference at the same time, DM us and we'll get you connected!
