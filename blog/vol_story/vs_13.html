Verve Society volunteered for over 1600 hours and created an impact of more than ₹ 06,00,000 for our partner NGO Umoya Sports Foundation

Here’s what they have to share about their experience -
"24 members of Verve recently volunteered with The Umoya Sports NGO, all thanks to our successful collaboration with ImpactHub. We are a street-play society looking to help where we can and do our part in these virtual times.

Social media and other platforms have become increasingly important in these times where everything has shifted online and such reach for NGOs can help in multiple ways. Hence to help them out we created content for the NGO in the form of reels, posts, promotional videos. We even helped the NGO with program management and content research as well.

As a group, we always wanted to help where we can and raise social awareness and this turned out to be a perfect way to do so even in the midst of Lockdown.We had a lot of fun collaborating over topics and videos and the NGO we worked with were really supportive and guided us throughout this volunteering stint.

Virtual Volunteering can make a huge difference in such tough times. When you have the skill and will to do something, helping such NGOs and people can actually translate into a lot. Why let physical distance stop you? Virtual Volunteering is a win-win situation for both the NGOs and the Volunteers.

To all the people who have not yet volunteered for a variety of reasons, please do so. You definitely should. It helps you grow and learn and contribute. Do your part. Help. Volunteer!"
