Our volunteer, Sidharth Dembi, has been working as a Math teacher with the Angel Express Foundation since 2017.

Published on 10 December, 2020



Story

Our volunteer, Sidharth Dembi, has been working as a Math teacher with the Angel Express Foundation since 2017. During this time, he has devoted more than 100 volunteering hours and brought with him numerous new opportunities to the NGO, such as preparing children for IMO Olympiads on weekends! Here’s what Sidharth has to say about his experience: “Thanks to ImpactHub, I got the opportunity to work with the Angel Express Foundation over the last two years and the experience has been extremely satisfying. I have been teaching Mathematics to kids who study in and between grades 3 to 6, and previously during regular weekly classes but now only on the weekends. Along with another volunteer, I helped prepare some children for the annual school level IMO maths exam, popularly known as the IMO Maths Olympiad, and it was a very rewarding experience to watch these kids make such good progress. I have found the children to be extremely enthusiastic and interested, and I myself eagerly await my time with them every week.” The NGO has described Sidharth as a treasure for their institution, and his relationship with the children as wonderful. ImpactHub is proud to provide individuals with not just volunteering opportunities, but also with the chance to build meaningful and lasting relationships.
