Story

Here is Binal's story. A mind and skill development specialist by profession,she made a difference by using her skills and conducted various Motivational Therapy sessions with our NGO partners. Here is what she wishes to share about her experience "I always wanted to do something which uplifts the community as a whole. I realised that if I have potential I should use to inculcate some positive change, hence volunteering gave me a headway to give back to the society. I believe that through the workshops that I facilitate, even if I can make a difference in one person’s life, it is all worth it. I came to know about ImpactHub through an acquaintance who guided to get ahead about volunteering. My experience with ImpactHub was like that one mentor for volunteering that I needed, filled with opportunities to pick and choose from, so that I can give 100% quality of work where I was required to. Considering the fact that Life Skills Coaching is my forte, I found the most fruitful opportunities on ImpactHub to facilitate interactive and meaningful workshops with the associated NGOs.In a span of 3 months, I have been associated with 2 NGOs – Bright Future India and Lighthouse Project and have undertaken 3 activity-based workshops
