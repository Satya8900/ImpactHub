Parinaaz did her bit by Volunteering in HR and has created a significant impact! Here is what she has to say about her experience - "I always wanted to volunteer for a social cause but due to work constraints, I never got the opportunity. Once I joined a 5-day working company I finally had the time on weekends. I started searching for NGOs where I could volunteer in my spare time and contribute to society. Since I have done my MBA in Human Resources I wanted to work with an NGO in that domain so that I can make the best use of my HR skills. ImpactHub connected me to Healing Dove which works towards reforming and rehabilitating marginalized youth groups. My work was to help them set up HR policies, Rules & Regulations, Organization culture etc. My experience was wonderful with the Healing Dove team.
The work that they are doing will really help the marginalized groups to make something out of their life. I’m looking forward to the chance to volunteer with ImpactHub again!" Be a Volunteer today DM us now and we will get you connected! .
