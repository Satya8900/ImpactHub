Sanidhya did his bit by volunteering to teach kids English and Maths and has created a significant impact!

Published on 10 December, 2020



Story

Sanidhya did his bit by volunteering to teach kids English and Maths and has created a significant impact! Here is what he has to say about his experience - "I had no plans for Saturday. All my friends wanted to go clubbing or to a fancy restaurant for dinner, I wasn’t in a mood for that. I was looking for something unique, something meaningful and I came across ImpactHub! That Saturday, I found about Bigger Than Life and their Juhu sessions. I registered as a volunteer and got a call from ImpactHub regarding this volunteering opportunity. I always wanted to be a part of an NGO but I thought I wouldn’t be able to give time because of my hectic schedule. wanted to contribute in whatever way possible.It makes it easy and convenient for you. I decided to volunteer whenever I can and I will keep on doing that. I will be very honest, I wasn’t as enthusiastic in the beginning as I am now. It drives you, it gives you a high that nothing else can give. ImpactHub has always reached me in a few days whenever I have shown interest in any activity and has connected me to the right opportunities. ImpactHub is really doing something special out there for genuine causes. With Bigger Than Life, I have been attending their Juhu Sessions regularly which involve playing with kids and teaching them some basic maths and English. These kids are from a nearby slum. I love these sessions because these kids make me really happy. I learn something new in each and every session.
Now my plan is to work with other NGOs as well so that I can contribute on a larger level. Thank you so much ImpactHub! Keep Connecting!" Does Sanidhya's story inspire you to start your volunteering journey? Reach out to us now and we will connect you :)⠀
