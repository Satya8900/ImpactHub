Meet Mirthu Bashini, a changemaker who has successfully completed her volunteering journey with our NGO Partner - Vivekananda Youth Forum.

Published on 22 July, 2021



Story

Meet Mirthu Bashini, a changemaker who has successfully completed her volunteering journey with our NGO Partner - Vivekananda Youth Forum. Mirthu has also been an intern at ImpactHub and an active volunteer for our various group virtual volunteering activities! Here’s what she has to say about her volunteering journey so far - “ImpactHub is one of the best things that has happened to me in 2020. I began volunteering at Connectfor in the month of September 2020 when I came across one of their events listed on Frapp. Since then, I have enrolled myself and participated in various volunteering programs as a volunteer. I am a second year student and my interest lies in the HR Field, based on which the ImpactHub team connected me with NGO Vivekananda Youth Forum. During my course of volunteering, I drafted around 10 policies on health, safety, leave etc for their organization. It was a little challenging at first as I had to frame these policies from scratch, but the NGO staff was very humble and supportive which made this volunteering journey with them a beautiful learning experience for me. I felt so comfortable while I volunteered with them. I feel that experiences like these considering the tough times we are going through due to this pandemic will go a long way. Volunteering virtually can benefit a lot and help make a difference, no matter what your geographical location is. In my case, I volunteered from my home in Coimbatore to help an NGO based in Mumbai make a difference! Trust me, volunteering will add value to your life and it will change you into a better person.”
