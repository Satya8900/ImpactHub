Story

Swapna Salian has successfully completed her Micro Volunteering journey with ImpactHub. Here’s what she has to say about her volunteering experience - “A couple of years ago, ImpactHub had organized a cricket tournament for NGOs. One of the NGOs was Udaan with whom I have been volunteering for the past 5 years. Our winning click was something I recently bumped upon and saw ImpactHub was the organizer. Curiosity took me to google search and here I am today a part of the volunteering community at ImpactHub. Volunteering gives me a sense of content and happiness that is rare to find. It feels wonderful to know that you can make a difference to the world around you. Surprisingly enough some of your fun hobbies could also become a part of volunteering and help someone. I really liked the part about Micro Volunteering where each activity called for an x number of things. For instance writing x number of letters or preparing x number of worksheets for kids. This x is something that keeps the option open for volunteers to contribute as little or as much as possible. So you can start small and do this in your break time or even over weekends. I hope that anyone who hasn't volunteered before takes a tiny step towards it and gets hooked to the sheer joy of giving back. Afterall, aren't we all here to make the world a better place than when we arrived?”
