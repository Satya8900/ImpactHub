Published on 22 July, 2021



Story

Meet Ayush Sarawagi, a young changemaker who volunteered recently and completed an administrative project with our NGO partner, Makkala Jagriti. Speaking about his volunteering experience so far Ayush says, “I came across ImpactHub’s social media handle while I was browsing for volunteering opportunities. The ImpactHub team has been very prompt in their response and connected me with the right opportunities. I volunteered with Makkala Jagriti lately, and helped them curate worksheets and assignments for kids from 4th, 5th, 7th & 8th grade. It was an amazing learning experience for me and the NGO was very supportive and helpful during my course of volunteering. As soon as I completed my volunteering project with Makkala Jagriti, I began volunteering with PIFA for market research and that’s one of the reasons I really loved volunteering through ImpactHub. They have a pool of opportunities that one can explore and volunteer for!”
