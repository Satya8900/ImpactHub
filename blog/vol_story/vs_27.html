Tamanna did her bit by Volunteering in Strategic planning, Brand Building and many more and has created a significant impact! Here is what she has to say about her experience - " I have been in the volunteering space for some time. I'm sensitive to a range of causes hence was looking for a platform who could connect me with a variety of them. My friend, a core team member of Connectfor introduced me to this platform. True to its name, 'connect for' gave me the opportunity to work for a range of issues such as Sports [PIFA], mental wellness [Artscape], juvenile homes [Healing Dove Foundation], branding and marketing [Raay Foundation], etc and still counting. I was looking for something where I could use my knowledge/expertise at a far more strategic level with the NGOs.ImpactHub understood this well. They connected me to the above NGO s above I created solutions to overcome their key challenges.
