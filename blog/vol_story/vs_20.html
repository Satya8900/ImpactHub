“The pandemic definitely triggered the search for volunteering opportunities,” says Ashwini Sawant

Published on 22 July, 2021



Story

“The pandemic definitely triggered the search for volunteering opportunities,” says Ashwini Sawant, our star volunteer for the month who successfully completed her volunteering journey with our NGO Partner - Jayavantrao Ghyar Patil Sevabhavi Sanstha. “I was looking forward to utilising my time in doing something meaningful and the ImpactHub website came very handy. It was very interesting as well as surprising to know that there are so many opportunities available to volunteer for, under various cause verticals. I found my match with Jayavantrao Ghyar Patil Sevabhavi Sanstha (JGPSS) in November 2020, and began my volunteering journey as a Blogging Volunteer. I submitted around 15-20 blogs in English and Marathi on topics like Organic Farming and its enormous benefits on Earth, Ecology and Human beings; etc. This opportunity proved to be very interesting and enriching with so many new things to learn about organic farming. I am really thankful to the ImpactHub team and Mr Chandrakant Patil who is heading JGPSS for the amazing work you do. Virtual volunteering is the need of the hour and to top it all, it's convenient. As per the COVID-19 guidelines, work from home was a new concept for most of us, and ImpactHub’s virtual volunteering program gave a unique touch to it. For me social work was going into the society, working on grassroot level but with virtual volunteering that definition has changed. It has made volunteering feasible by staying at home with my family, staying safe and still contributing to the society at large! I would personally want to encourage people to volunteer their time and skills for a cause to make a difference!”
