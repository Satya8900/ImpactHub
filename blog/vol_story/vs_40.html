Waseem was a volunteer with the ImpactHub team, and quickly became a highly contributing and impactful peer for us!

Published on 10 December, 2020



Story

Waseem was a volunteer with the ImpactHub team, and quickly became a highly contributing and impactful peer for us! This is what Waseem has to say about his experience: "I volunteered with ImpactHub as a strategy consultant. As I had worked in the Strategy team of a large corporation, I was able to create plans for their various events as well as their financial budget for the year. I implemented a performance tracking system for them to monitor their progress against the goals they had set for the year. Apart from being a volunteer at ImpactHub, I have also volunteered with Teach A Class foundation where I created a system for them to manage all the applications they receive from teachers so that they can speedily provide tech solutions where they are needed most. My impact for ImpactHub and Teach A Class Foundation was significant in terms of the value I was able to generate, since I worked with both organizations pro bono. The best part about it was that I was enjoying volunteering so much that I didn't even notice the hours add up! My most memorable volunteering experience was at ImpactHub when I was conducting a 2-day training session which took longer than expected to complete. The team unanimously agreed to stay back beyond work hours to complete the session, and I saw right then how much they valued the time and effort I was putting into the project!‚Äù
