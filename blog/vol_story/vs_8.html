Featuring today on Mother's Day is the testimonial of our volunteer Pallavi Khurana, a Corporate Soft Skills Trainer and a mother of 2

Published on 09 May, 2023



Story

Featuring today on Mother's Day is the testimonial of our volunteer Pallavi Khurana, a Corporate Soft Skills Trainer and a mother of 2 lovely teenage daughters, Pallavi Khurana

Here's what she has to say about managing kids and volunteering -"I have taken a short break from my Training profession and want to do a few things, volunteering for an NGO being one of them. My younger daughter encouraged me to be a volunteer.

Currently, I'm volunteering for New Mighty Grace Charitable Trust to help them frame policies for their employees, and recently joined the ImpactHub & Action Earth India team for a school painting event with my daughters!

I feel grateful and privileged every single day and feel I can do my bit for people who are not. Even my daughter has been taking English classes for underprivileged kids through ImpactHub.

I feel Moms who volunteer not only make this world a better place but raise sensitive, emphatic, helpful, and responsible citizens and we need such a conscious new generation to come up.

Here's my two-bit for people & moms, especially those who have not volunteered before -

We work tirelessly for our loved ones,kids ,family but doing something for some stranger is far more rewarding. Find some time to volunteer in any way as nothing is more satisfying than that million dollar smile in return when you do something for someone unconditionally."
